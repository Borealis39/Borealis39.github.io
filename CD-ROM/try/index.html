 This is a collection of materials related to the original manga "Try! Try! Try!".
Originally, this production was planned to be published specifically for inclusion in Azumanga 2;
however, to introduce this work, a few illustrations and webcomics were first posted to the internet.

The first webcomic produced is known as "Ena's Misfortune".
It's a story about the three sisters of the Ayase family who live next door to the main character.
Here is a revised version with corrections and improvements compared to what was originally uploaded to the internet.
Even though Yotsuba is the main character, she does not appear in this story.
Since I wanted to show that this was a supplementary chapter, I have numbered it "Chapter Negative 1".

The next published webcomic is the "Fractional Edition".
This is an omnibus where I have drawn Yotsuba and the people around her.
Since the last supplementary chapter's number was negative, and this comic was an omnibus of several smaller vignettes,
I have named it the "Fractional Edition".
